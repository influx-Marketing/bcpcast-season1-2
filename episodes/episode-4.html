About Databarracks’ Business Continuity Podcast

Business continuity and disaster recovery are often maligned as dry, academic areas of study. Throughout a series of interviews with leading continuity practitioners, the Databarracks BCPcast takes a more human approach.

Continuity is both simpler than you might think, and incredibly easy to get wrong in the heat of the moment. We’ll explore how people and organisations have recovered from some of the most infamous disasters in recent history, along with a few weird ones for good measure.

The Business Continuity Podcast is funded, promoted and made by Databarracks.

Episode 4

Plans Are Useless, Planning Is Everything

Listen on iTunes

In the fourth episode of the BCPcast, we’re going to look at the point at which business continuity and disaster recovery stop existing as ideas and start to take shape as actions, as we examine the very practical process of planning for the worst, and the testing that ensures those plans work.

How should you structure planning sessions? Who should you invite? And what obstacles and outcomes can you expect?
